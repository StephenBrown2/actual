# The Sidebar

### Sidebar

The sidebar in Actual holds all of the links to navigate around the application, we will break them down below.

<Tabs groupId="device">
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>

On desktop, the sidebar is on the left and shows links to Budget, Accounts, Reports, Schedules, and more. Click any link to switch views.

![](/img/using-actual/budget-sidebar.webp)

  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">

On mobile, navigation is in the bottom bar. Tap **Budget**, **Accounts**, **Reports**, or the **More** menu to open other sections. The same links are available in a compact layout.

![](/img/using-actual/budget-sidebar-mobile.webp)

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

### Account List

The sidebar also holds all of your accounts both On budget and Off budget, these will display in a list under the heading they belong e.g. On budget. You can find out how to add an account [here](../accounts/index.md#adding-a-new-account)

<Tabs groupId="device">
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>

![](/img/using-actual/budget-sidebar-accounts.webp)

  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">

On mobile, tap **Accounts** in the bottom nav to see the account list, then tap an account to open its register.

![](/img/using-actual/budget-sidebar-accounts-mobile.webp)

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
